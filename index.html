About Kommie-keeb

Kommie-keeb is a custom keyboard designed and built by Souptik Samanta. Crafted with precision and passion, it features a unique ANSI layout and high-quality components for a superior typing experience.

Gallery

Bill of Materials (BOM)

  • Display: SSD1306 OLED Display, 0.96", I2C interface
  • Switches: Cherry MX Mechanical Switches (88 pcs)
  • Keycaps & Layout: ANSI layout featuring various key sizes
  • Stabilizers: For oversized keys such as Backspace, Enter, and Shifts
  • Passive Components: Resistors (4.7kΩ, 5.1kΩ) and LEDs
  • Connectors & PCB: USB-C connector and custom PCB ("Soup_pad")
